## Redefining Intelligent Education

Existing AI education products share three core flaws: generic AI lacks teaching logic; institutions offer one-size-fits-all content; traditional classrooms have 40:1 student-teacher ratios with no personalization.

**Otowyue Learn AI** puts emotion sensing at the core, FSRS scientific memory as the engine, and VRM digital humans as the interface — creating an "AI teacher with soul" that makes every student feel understood and seen.
