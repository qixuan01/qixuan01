## Core Advantages

### LiFePO₄ Chemistry
Mature and stable LiFePO₄ technology with excellent thermal stability, no risk of thermal runaway, far superior safety compared to ternary lithium batteries.

### Ultra-Long Cycle Life
Cycle life **≥ 10,000 times** — at one full cycle per day, this exceeds 27 years of use.

### IP66 All-Weather Protection
Full dust and strong water-jet protection, suitable for outdoor installation in any weather.

### Modular Flexible Expansion
Single or multiple units (up to 6) in parallel, flexibly scaling from 6kWh to 54kWh+.

### Multi-Layer Safety
Built-in BMS battery management system with cell-level overcharge, over-discharge, over-temperature, and short-circuit protection, plus AFCI arc detection.
