## Solution Overview

Designed for large homes and villas. Large-capacity PV combined with large-capacity storage, plus V2G bidirectional EV charging, achieves near-complete energy self-sufficiency and enables participation in VPP programs for additional revenue.

## Standard Configuration

| Component | Model | Specification |
|-----------|-------|--------------|
| Hybrid Inverter | Qixuan Hybrid Three-Phase | 12.0 kW |
| Energy Storage | Qixuan BAT 10.0 × 4 | Total 36 kWh |
| PV Modules | Standard Monocrystalline | 16 kWp |
| EV Charger | Qixuan EVDC 7kW | With V2G |
| Communication | Qixuan CommMod | 5G/WiFi |

## Key Features

- **V2G Bidirectional Charging** — EV participates in home energy storage, provides emergency backup during outages
- **Three-Phase High Power** — Supports central air conditioning, electric water heaters and other high-power appliances
- **Whole-Home Energy Management** — AI system unified scheduling, prioritizing PV and storage power

## Economic Benefits

- **Annual Generation**: ~18,000 to 20,000 kWh
- **Annual Electricity Savings**: ~$1,500 to $2,500
- **Estimated Payback Period**: 5 to 8 years
