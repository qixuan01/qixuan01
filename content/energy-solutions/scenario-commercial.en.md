## Solution Overview

Designed for small factories, shops, and office buildings. The solar-storage system charges during off-peak hours and discharges during peak hours, effectively reducing basic electricity fees and kWh charges while providing UPS-level power backup for critical equipment.

| Component | Model | Specification |

| Energy Storage | StarKernal BAT 10.0 × 5 | Total 45 kWh |

| Communication | StarKernal CommMod | 4G |

## Peak-Valley Arbitrage Model

Based on typical commercial tariff spread (~$0.15/kWh):

- **Daily arbitrage volume**: ~40 kWh
- **Annual arbitrage revenue**: ~$2,000 to $2,400
- **Combined annual benefit (incl. PV savings)**: ~$4,000 to $5,000
- **Estimated payback period**: **4 to 6 years**
- **25-year total return**: Significant positive ROI
