## Solution Overview

Designed for ordinary residential homes, combining PV generation with energy storage to significantly reduce grid dependence, achieve green low-carbon living, and substantially save on electricity costs.

## System Operation Logic

**Daytime**: PV generation supplies home consumption first, surplus stored in batteries, excess exported to grid.

**Nighttime**: Batteries discharge to power home, grid supplements when battery is insufficient.

**Power Outage**: Automatic switchover to off-grid within 10ms, batteries maintain continuous supply.
