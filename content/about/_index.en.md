# About Shenzhen Qixuan Software Co., Ltd.

## Company Overview

Shenzhen Qixuan Software Co., Ltd. is a technology company specializing in big data infrastructure, AI education, and edge AI inference. Headquartered in Longgang District, Shenzhen, the company follows a "self-developed, full-stack controllable" technical route and carries the mission of domestic substitution. Its four core product lines cover big data intelligent operations, AI education innovation, edge AI inference acceleration, and smart terminal middleware — with over 80,000 lines of fully proprietary code.

Core technology routes: Apache Ambari Ecosystem · RISC-V+FPGA Heterogeneous Computing · AI Digital Human Education

## Company Milestones

### 2018 — Founded
Shenzhen Qixuan Software Co., Ltd. established in Longgang, Shenzhen, focusing on self-developed big data infrastructure software

### 2023 — HadoopForge Project Launch
Initiated development of enterprise Hadoop cluster intelligent O&M platform, filling the Ambari+Bigtop version gap

### 2024 — Multi-line Breakthrough
Launched Hongyuan edge AI inference accelerator R&D; Otowyue Learn AI education platform completed core architecture

### 2025 — Award Recognition
Hongyuan Edge AI Inference Accelerator won the "Architecture Innovation Award" at the 2025 Shenzhen RISC-V and HarmonyOS Innovation Competition; HadoopForge obtained software copyright registration

### 2026 — Commercialization Acceleration
HadoopForge completed 3 seed customer validations: 500+ nodes, 12 months, 0 production incidents; initiated angel round fundraising

## <span id="products">Four Core Product Lines</span>

### HadoopForge — Big Data Intelligent O&M
Enterprise-grade Hadoop cluster full lifecycle management platform. First in China to complete Ambari 3.0.0 + Bigtop 3.5.0 adaptation. AI-powered fault prediction accuracy of 92%.

### Otowyue Learn AI — AI Education Innovation
Fully offline + open-source + VRM digital human education AI platform. FSRS scientific memory + BKT knowledge graph. 60,000+ lines of production-grade code. Compatible with 11+ LLMs.

### Hongyuan Edge AI — Edge Inference Acceleration
World's first BitNet FPGA accelerator. Full-stack RISC-V+FPGA+HarmonyOS LiteOS. BOM cost <¥200. 100% domestically sourced. 256×256 matrix zero error.

### Smart Terminal AI — Intelligent Terminal Middleware
Edge AI tiered inference platform for IoT / industrial embedded scenarios. Three-tier progressive inference architecture. Full AI running in 606KB SRAM. Supports any RISC-V/ARM MCU.
