- **Flight Trajectory Analysis** - Precise flight trajectory calculation and analysis
- **Performance Parameter Monitoring** - Real-time flight performance monitoring
- **Anomaly Detection Warning** - Intelligent flight anomaly detection
- **Optimization Recommendations** - Flight plan optimization suggestions

### Equipment Health Management
- **Equipment Condition Monitoring** - Aerospace equipment health monitoring
- **Predictive Maintenance** - Equipment fault prediction analysis
- **Lifecycle Assessment** - Equipment service life assessment
- **Maintenance Plan Optimization** - Intelligent maintenance planning

### Mission Planning Support
- **Orbit Calculation Analysis** - Precise orbit calculation and analysis
- **Resource Scheduling Optimization** - Intelligent mission resource scheduling
- **Risk Assessment Analysis** - Mission risk assessment and warning
- **Decision Support System** - Intelligent mission decision support
